============================================
Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program (MPNP)
============================================

The MPNP allows Manitoba to nominate individuals for permanent residence who have the skills and experience needed in the local labor market.

Program Streams
---------------

- **Skilled Worker in Manitoba Stream**: For workers currently working in Manitoba with a permanent job offer.
- **Skilled Worker Overseas Stream**: For skilled workers who can demonstrate a strong connection to Manitoba.
- **International Education Stream**: For international graduates from Manitoba institutions.
- **Business Investor Stream**: For individuals intending to start or purchase a business in Manitoba.

Application Process
-------------------

1. **Submit an Expression of Interest (EOI)**: Indicate your interest in applying to the MPNP.
2. **Receive an Invitation to Apply (ITA)**: Selected candidates receive an ITA based on their EOI score.
3. **Submit Application**: Complete and submit the application within the given timeframe.
4. **Nomination**: If successful, receive a nomination to apply for permanent residence.

For more details, visit the official MPNP website: ([immigratemanitoba.com](https://www.immigratemanitoba.com/))
