========================================
Nova Scotia Nominee Program (NSNP)
========================================

The NSNP allows Nova Scotia to nominate individuals for permanent residence who have the skills and experience needed in the province.

Program Streams
---------------

- **Nova Scotia Experience: Express Entry**: For skilled individuals with at least one year of work experience in Nova Scotia.
- **Nova Scotia Labour Market Priorities**: For candidates with an Express Entry profile who meet specific labor market needs.
- **Skilled Worker**: For individuals with a job offer from a Nova Scotia employer.
- **Physician**: For internationally trained general practitioners, family physicians, and specialist physicians with a job offer in Nova Scotia.
