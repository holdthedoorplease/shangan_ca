============================================
Alberta Advantage Immigration Program (AAIP)
============================================

The AAIP allows Alberta to nominate individuals for permanent residence who have the skills and abilities to fill labor shortages in the province.

Program Streams
---------------

- **Alberta Opportunity Stream**: For foreign workers who are already working full-time in Alberta and have a job offer from an Alberta employer.
- **Alberta Express Entry Stream**: For candidates with an Express Entry profile who have strong ties to Alberta or who can help support the government's economic development and diversification priorities.
- **Alberta Accelerated Tech Pathway**: For Express Entry candidates working in Alberta's tech industry.
- **Rural Renewal Stream**: For workers with a job offer from an employer in a designated rural Alberta community.

Application Process
-------------------

1. **Determine Eligibility**: Review the specific requirements for each stream.
2. **Submit an Expression of Interest (EOI)**: For certain streams, submit an EOI to indicate your interest.
3. **Receive a Notification of Interest (NOI)**: Selected candidates receive an NOI from the province.
4. **Submit Application**: Complete and submit the application within the specified timeframe.
5. **Nomination**: If approved, receive a provincial nomination to apply for permanent residence.

For detailed information, visit the official AAIP website: ([alberta.ca](https://www.alberta.ca/aaip.aspx))
