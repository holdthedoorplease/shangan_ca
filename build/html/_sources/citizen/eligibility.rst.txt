=========================
Apply for Citizenship
=========================

Applying for Canadian citizenship is a significant step. Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria and follow the application process carefully.

Eligibility Requirements
-------------------------

To become a Canadian citizen, you must:

- Be a permanent resident.
- Have lived in Canada for at least 3 out of the last 5 years (1,095 days).
- Have filed your taxes, if required.
- Pass a citizenship test.
- Prove your language skills in English or French.
- Take the oath of citizenship.

Other requirements may apply. For detailed information, visit the official IRCC page: https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/canadian-citizenship/become-canadian-citizen.html

Application Process
-------------------

1. **Prepare Your Application:**
   - Gather necessary documents.
   - Complete the application form.
   - Pay the application fees.

2. **Submit Your Application:**
   - Apply online or by mail, depending on your eligibility.

3. **After Applying:**
   - Wait for the acknowledgment of receipt.
   - Prepare for the citizenship test and interview.

For more details, refer to the IRCC guide: https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/canadian-citizenship/become-canadian-citizen/apply.html
